Meet Claritza

Her savory and sweet creations have made special celebrations even more spectacular for hundreds of people of from different schools, businesses, churches and non-profit organizations in the City of Los Angeles. Claritza is a cake-making extraordinaire and will masterfully bring your confectionary visions to life! Her clients are her inspiration, so send her a request online for a free consultation to discuss your custom order! Claritza Mendez is the artist and pâtissier behind Cakes by Claritza. Ever since she received an E-Z Bake Oven at the age of 8, Claritza has been fascinated with baking and knew that one day she’d have a bakery of her own. She was formally trained at the prestigious Le Cordon Bleu College of Culinary Arts in Pasadena, CA.

After graduation, she immediately began her apprenticeship at one of Los Angeles’ most reputable bakeries where she perfected her design, sculpting, viennoiserie-making and cake decorating skills in a real world environment. Claritza’s confections are not only beautiful but superior in taste as well. Whether it be a Red Velvet, Vanilla, Chocolate, Strawberry, Marble, Carrot, Lemon flavored cake or a custom caked built using your favorite flavors, she will create a work-of-cake-art that will not only impress your guests but will also create unforgettable memories.
